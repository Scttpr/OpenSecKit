> Effective: 27 June 2021
> Stability: Stable

---

## Overview

The 2021 SCCs replace the previous versions (2001, 2004, 2010) and must be used for new contracts. Existing contracts had until 27 December 2022 to transition.

## Supplementary Measures

Following Schrems II (CJEU C-311/18), supplementary measures may be required:

### Technical Measures
- End-to-end encryption
- Pseudonymisation
- Split processing

### Organisational Measures
- Internal policies
- Transparency reports
- Data subject notification

### Contractual Measures
- Enhanced audit rights
- Warrant canary clauses
